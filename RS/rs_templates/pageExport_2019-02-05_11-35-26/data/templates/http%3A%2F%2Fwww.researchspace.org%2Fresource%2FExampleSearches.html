Boolean Text Search

In this text search we use parenthesis to search for both Louis XIV and Louis XV.
When entering text in the search box, we type 

(xiv or xv) and louis
The section in brackets is a search subquery, searching for either 'xiv' OR 'xv'. We then use AND to specify that any results from the subquery must also include 'louis'.

In effect we have conducted two searches: the first for 'xiv' OR 'xv'; and the second for occurrences of 'louis' in the results of the first search.
